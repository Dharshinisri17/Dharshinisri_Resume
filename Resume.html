Route Master

Route Master is a public transportation management website that displays the timings and availability of buses in specific areas. Users can enter their location and desired destination to find available bus routes, bus timings, and estimated travel times. The website helps commuters plan their trips more efficiently by providing real-time updates on bus availability and schedules.

Agro Master

Agro Master is an agricultural assistance website designed to help farmers by providing information on the best crops to grow based on the season, soil type, and region. The website guides farmers on when to cultivate crops, the necessary steps to ensure healthy growth, and recommends fertilizers and seeds. Additionally, farmers can purchase fertilizers, seeds, and other agricultural supplies directly through the platform, making it a one-stop solution for their farming needs.
